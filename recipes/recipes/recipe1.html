Bill's Bug Bonanza

This appetising meal is courtesy of our good - and not at all malevolent - friend, Bill.

Bill is a hell of a guy (take that how you will). He would never do anything to jeopardise the health and well being of the billions of people. You can ignore any of the pernicious rumours you have heard about him from those crazy conspiracy theorists. What's more, Bill can't be bad because he is the founder of a Tech Giant and has made gazillions of dollars. Plus, who ever heard anything bad about those kinda guys... With all that being said, you are probably excited to learn how to conjure this delicious meal. Well, Bill actually learned how to make this dish from his good friend Jeffrey while holidaying on his private island. Sadly, Jeffrey wrote the instructions on a secret list and that list has mysteriously disappeared - along with Jeffrey.
